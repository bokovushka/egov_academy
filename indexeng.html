Why E-government?

E-government makes it possible for any citizen to use electronic services to receive government services online. Thanks to Egov, you can get almost any government service staying home.

Egov will help to spare a great sum for the budget of the country and optimize resources. So the provision of e-services has become one of the priority directions of the work of the Ukrainian government.

EGOV Academy created on the basis of the aggregator of all-Ukrainian and regional electronic government services Egov.in.ua. The academy team selects only relevant topics and experienced speakers to make digitalization training as effective as possible.

Our areas of study

Cybersecurity

Ukraine should have a strict standard for the storage, and protection of personal data of everyone.

Teaching digitization

We are struggling with the fact that 54% of Ukrainians don’t have digital skills of using the Internet.

Implementation in cities and regions

Everyone can create an e-government service. The main thing is the desire to make the world digital.

Foreign experience

We adopt the experience of the leading countries in the development of e-gov and adapt it in Ukraine.

Egov Academy is created for:

Representatives of the city and regional authorities

The city and regional authorities will be able to learn how to create e-government services for their city or region.

Representatives of the central government

Representatives of the central government will be able to learn how to create e-government services and scale them to cities and regions.

Developers

Developers will be able to learn to identify the needs and problems that can be solved with the help of e-government services and master the mechanism of their creation.

Everyone who wants to create an e-service

Anyone can create an e-government service. The main desire is to take the initiative into their own hands.
